MyTravel App

Real World B2C Travel Site

View the Travel Site

In the Scottish Travel Industry there is a shortage of tech that encourages customers to engage with the product they have purchased.

I created this website to bridge the gap between purchase and service.

In order to encourage retention and social interaction, I made this app to not only showcase the product they had purchased, but also as a means to build anticipation before joining a tour and introduce a social element.

Note: This is a real-life, ongoing project.

Languages Used:

A full stack application: MySQL, Node.js, React, SCSS, HTML, API integration
